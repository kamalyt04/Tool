How to Compress Images

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking "Select Image"
  2. Adjust the quality slider to your preferred compression level
  3. Choose output format (or keep original format)
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  5. Download your optimized image with reduced file size

Why Compress Images?

Image compression offers several benefits:

  • Faster website loading - Smaller images load quicker, improving user experience and SEO
  • Reduced bandwidth usage - Save on hosting costs and mobile data consumption
  • Better storage efficiency - Store more images without using excessive space
  • Improved social media sharing - Many platforms compress images anyway, so better to control the quality yourself

Supported Image Formats

Our tool supports all major image formats:

  • JPG/JPEG - Best for photographs and complex images
  • PNG - Ideal for graphics with transparency
  • WebP - Modern format offering superior compression
